About Me

Music is my lifelong passion. I began studying harp, piano, and voice at six and first performed professionally at eleven. I have performed extensively at notable venues including the Edgewater Hotel in Seattle, the Avalon Theater in Hollywood, and the Kahala Resort in Honolulu.

Recently I have traveled internationally as the principal harpist aboard Cunard's luxury cruise ship The Queen Elizabeth. I toured Scandanavia and the Mediterranean while entertaining cruise passengers as a solo artist and as part of the onboard orchestra. When I am not traveling or performing, I teach harp, piano, and voice. Passing on the gift of music to others, helping them achieve their goals and build self esteem is incredibly rewarding.
